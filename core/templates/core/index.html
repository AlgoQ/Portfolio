Full Stack Developer / Quant

Always learning, always growing.

From humble beginnings with HTML & CSS, where I recreated designs of popular websites like Netflix, my journey as a software developer started. On this journey, I've built numerous personal projects, both public and private.

Recently, I successfully launched one of my trading strategies as a vault on Hyperliquid. I also recreated the website of my favorite book, "The Almanack of Naval Ravikant," as a tribute to its impact on me.

When I step away from the screen, you'll find me walking my dog, enjoying a refreshing yerba mate, or immersed in a good book.
